--- subject: The Skye Coastal Chair Debuts in Cashiers ---

A New Bruce Andrews Design Introduction

Our new Skye Coastal chair, a slimmer version of our Highland chair, will make its first appearance at the Cashiers Historical Society Showhouse from August 13 through 28 in the space designed by Ginger Brewton Interiors.

“Our inspiration for this new addition to Collection Skye, beyond a coastal aesthetic, was to create chairs for Ginger’s interiors that reflected luxuriousness while remaining naturally livable. The Libeco Belgian linen and Celadon hues achieve these aspirations beautifully.”
